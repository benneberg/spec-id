SYSCARDS

System Card Guide

What is a System Card?

  • Concise documentation of a product or system.
  • Core inclusions: problem, solution, workflow, differentiators, monetization, and handoff readiness.

Best Practices

  • Be relentlessly clear and concise.
  • Focus heavily on what makes your product uniquely valuable.
  • Include "what works / what doesn't" to honestly guide future steps.

Product Type Tips

  • AI Tool: Map the workflow, note deterministic vs. probabilistic logic areas.
  • Media/Video: Define the pipeline, export formats, and UX insights.
  • Dev Platform: Detail the API, onboarding flow, and integration points.
  • Game: Outline mechanics, player experience, and monetization loops.

Goal:

Make the card understandable in <2 minutes for someone brand new to the project.
